About Me

I am a researcher in computational mechanics, with a particular interest in the computational modelling of dynamic fracture. I am passionate about science, literature, and art. My research focuses on understanding how materials fail and how computational methods can be used to capture and predict fracture behaviour.

Literature has always been my first love. I am deeply drawn to classic, philosophical, and existential writers such as Dostoevsky, Kafka, Gogol, and Nietzsche. I write occasionally in Malayalam and English, and some of my writings can be found on my blog.

Painting, for me, is a happy, meditative space. Some of my artwork can be found on my Instagram page.
