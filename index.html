About Me

I am a researcher working in computational mechanics. I am passionate about science, literature, and art. My research explores the phenomenon of fracture through computational modelling, with a particular focus on heterogeneous solids. I am interested in understanding how materials fail, and how computational methods can be used to accurately capture and predict the phenomenon of fracture.

Literature has always been my first love. I am deeply drawn to classic, philosophical, and existential writers such as Dostoevsky, Kafka, Gogol, and Nietzsche. I write occasionally in Malayalam and English, and you can find some of my writings on my blog.

Painting, for me, is a happy, meditative space where the “I” fades, and only the tranquillity created by brushstrokes exists. Some of my artwork can be found on my Instagram page.
